
- World building system

Make Keys of Shadow Module: "The town is shrouded in darkness because a gnome has created a machine that is blocking the sun, the guild of tinkerers have tried and failed to fix it. Now, we need a party of adventurers to retrieve the 8 keys of the gnomes to unlock the machine and shut it down so they can see sunlight again." The town is always in darkness, but the party can use the normal torch or effect solutions to darkness as needed.

This world should be fairly large, the two should have a machine with eight slots that need artifacts. The dungeons should get progressively more difficult from 1 to 8. A level 1 dungeon would have one floor with level 1 monsters, a level two dungeon would have two levels with the first level populated by level 1 monsters and the second level with level two monsters and so on. The adventure ends when all 8 keys are inserted into the machine slots.
