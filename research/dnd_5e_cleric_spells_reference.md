# D&D 5e Cleric Spell Reference

A reference of Cleric spells from the D&D 5th Edition System Reference Document (SRD),
organized by spell level. Intended as inspiration for Realm of Shadow cleric/priest spells.

> **Key:** (R) = Ritual spell | School abbreviations: Abj = Abjuration, Con = Conjuration,
> Div = Divination, Enc = Enchantment, Evo = Evocation, Nec = Necromancy, Tra = Transmutation

---

## Notes for Realm of Shadow Adaptation

Spells most suitable for a retro Ultima III-style combat system:

**Direct damage (good for battle):** Sacred Flame, Guiding Bolt, Inflict Wounds, Spiritual Weapon, Spirit Guardians, Flame Strike, Harm, Fire Storm, Divine Word

**Healing (already partially implemented):** Cure Wounds, Healing Word, Prayer of Healing, Mass Healing Word, Mass Cure Wounds, Heal, Mass Heal

**Buffs (good for tactical combat):** Bless, Shield of Faith, Aid, Enhance Ability, Protection from Energy, Death Ward, Freedom of Movement, Holy Aura

**Debuffs (good for tactical combat):** Bane, Command, Blindness/Deafness, Hold Person, Bestow Curse, Banishment, Contagion

**Undead interaction (fits Turn Undead theme):** Spare the Dying, Gentle Repose, Animate Dead, Create Undead, Revivify, Raise Dead, Resurrection

**Utility (for overworld/dungeon use):** Detect Magic, Light, Create Food and Water, Remove Curse, Dispel Magic, Lesser/Greater Restoration
